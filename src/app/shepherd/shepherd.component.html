Shepherds Hut

Description

Our bookable shepherd's hut in Ottery St Mary is the perfect getaway for those seeking a tranquil escape surrounded by nature. Located on a working farm, our hut offers breathtaking views of lush green fields and grazing sheep.
Inside, the hut is a cozy retreat with a fully-equipped kitchen, a washer for your convenience, and a comfortable living area complete with a TV. And when it's time to turn in for the night, you'll find a snug sleeping area with a comfortable double bed and soft, warm bedding.
But the real beauty of our shepherd's hut is the great outdoors. Spend your days exploring the farm and the surrounding countryside, taking long walks along picturesque paths and keeping an eye out for wildlife. And at night, gather around the fire pit with loved ones and marvel at the starry skies above.
With free parking right outside the hut, you can easily explore the charming town of Ottery St Mary or take a trip to the nearby seaside town of Sidmouth. Whether you're looking for a romantic retreat, a solo adventure, or a family getaway, our shepherd's hut is the perfect base for your Ottery St Mary experience. Book your stay today and reconnect with nature in comfort and style.

Booking & Surroundings

The town of Ottery St Mary, on the outskirts of which this property is located, enjoys a fantastic location surrounded by countryside, and yet just a fifteen minute drive from the city of Exeter. ‘Ottery’ itself offers a range of independent shops, and a choice of eateries which pride themselves on serving the best of local produce. It is also quite unique, in that it still celebrates a number of traditional festivals, including Pixie day in June, and the Tar Barrells festival in November, where local men run through the town’s streets carrying flaming barrels of tar. The area surrounding the town has much to offer holiday makers, including the beach at Sidmouth, Wildwood Wildlife Park at Escot House, and Crealy Adventure Park.
